Let's make better tech together

Hello, I'm Alex!

A UX/UI Designer based in LATAM.

I create data-driven solutions that elevate human experiences

It's said that every chaos has a hidden order, and my brain concurs. I elevate innovation by merging my 9-year experience in advertising as a creative art director with tech solutions. My approach to user design is to focus intensely on aesthetics and data while making sure users always feel as if they’re gliding through my creations. I have a knack for combining beauty and usability while staying attuned to functionality and user needs to solve problems in the best way possible.

I would definitely work with him again."
