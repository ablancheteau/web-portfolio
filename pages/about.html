Hi! I'm Alexander Blancheteau

A passionate user experience (UX) designer who focuses on user-centered design and creating innovative solutions that meet the needs and expectations of both users and businesses. Currently, I work as a UX/UI Designer in the E-commerce department at Migrante, where I apply the user-centered design methodology to create exceptional user experiences that align with business goals.

My previous experience

In my previous experience as a UX/UI Designer at Entel, my main focus was to advise and connect technical teams with user needs and client expectations. I ensured that the user experience was simplified and that sales were maximized through digital channels by evaluating technical limitations and market requirements to find practical and implementable solutions for the company.

Before my career in UX/UI, I worked as a Senior Art Director in advertising and marketing agencies, leading branding projects and overseeing the implementation and supervision of campaigns for brands such as LATAM Airlines, L'Oréal, and Maybelline.

My experience in creating and developing on/off advertising campaigns for financial, real estate, alcoholic beverage, and retail brands has provided me with skills in managing multidisciplinary and collaborative projects, as well as understanding the needs and expectations of clients and users.

I'm always on the move

Looking for opportunities to continue growing in the field of user-centered design, exploring new solutions and technologies, and working on exciting projects with talented and motivated teams. If you want to learn more about my experience and skills, please do not hesitate to contact me. So, what are you waiting for? Let's make technology a bit better together!
