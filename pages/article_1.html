About the project

The current issue revolves around individuals maintaining their pets' medical records in a physical format. This often leads to information being easily lost, with many relying on memory to stay up-to-date with vaccinations and veterinary check-ups.

Moreover, the arrival of COVID-19 has impacted people's lifestyles and the capacity of veterinary clinics, making it necessary to be able to remotely schedule medical appointments.

The goal is to centralize our pets' medical records, annual check-up reminders, vaccination schedules, etc., along with information about nearby veterinary clinics, and the ability to request appointments, ensuring accessibility at all times.

The solution involves developing an app that allows users to keep their pets' profiles/records up to date, receive updates/notifications, schedule appointments at vet clinics, and provide reviews about these establishments.

Minimum Viable Product

Develop an app that allows for keeping your pets' profile/records updated, with the capability to receive updates/notifications, schedule appointments at veterinary clinics, and leave reviews about these establishments.

"PAWid is an app that will assist you in maintaining your pet's medical history in an easy, organized manner, always at your fingertips"

The App itself

After a thorough user testing phase involving a sample of five diverse participants, the application underwent a meticulous assessment. While the overall performance was deemed satisfactory, minor issues were highlighted across the user experience.

These findings, although not critical, prompted a deeper review and subsequent refinement. As a result, a strategic decision was made to introduce error screens, enhancing the app's capability to address unforeseen scenarios in line with best practices and heuristic guidelines. This iterative process aimed to fortify the app's compliance and resilience, providing users with a more seamless and secure interaction within the application ecosystem.

In conclusion, the design of this app can be considered a success. Overall, user feedback indicates a high level of satisfaction and contentment with its use. Users appear to be genuinely delighted and content with the application's functionalities, reflecting the success of the design in meeting their needs and providing an enjoyable and efficient user experience.
